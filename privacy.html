Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-20

MomentumShotz is operated by a single individual and is not a multi-user service. It does not collect data from, or about, the general public.

What data is accessed

The tool uses OAuth 2.0 to connect to the operator's own YouTube and TikTok accounts. It accesses only what's needed to publish video content to those connected accounts on the operator's behalf, plus basic public creator profile information (such as display name) used to confirm which account a post is about to go to before publishing. Access credentials are kept private and are never shared, sold, or transferred to any third party.

What data is not collected

The tool does not collect data about TikTok or YouTube viewers, followers, or any third party. It is not used to log in, authenticate, or identify any user other than the operator's own connected accounts.

Data retention

Access is retained only as long as needed to operate the pipeline. Revoking the connected app's authorization from a YouTube or TikTok account's settings cuts off the tool's access immediately.
