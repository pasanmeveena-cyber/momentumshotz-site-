Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-20

MomentumShotz is operated by a single individual and is not a multi-user service. It does not collect data from, or about, the general public.

What data is accessed

The tool uses OAuth 2.0 to connect to the operator's own YouTube and TikTok accounts. It stores, locally and in encrypted CI secrets it controls, only the access/refresh tokens needed to publish video content to those accounts on the operator's behalf, plus basic public creator profile fields (such as display name) returned by the TikTok Content Posting API's Query Creator Info endpoint, used solely to confirm which account a post is about to be published to before publishing.

What data is not collected

The tool does not collect data about TikTok or YouTube viewers, followers, or any third party. It does not sell, share, or transfer any data to any third party. It is not used to log in, authenticate, or identify any user other than the operator's own connected accounts.

Data retention

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the pipeline and are stored in gitignored local files or the operator's own CI secret store. Deleting the connected app's authorization from a YouTube or TikTok account's settings revokes the tool's access immediately.
