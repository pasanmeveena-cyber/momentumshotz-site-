MomentumShotz ("the tool", "we", "our") is an automated content pipeline operated by a single individual for personal, non-commercial publication of curated motivational short-form video clips to YouTube and TikTok.

What the tool does

The tool discovers long-form Creative Commons-licensed motivational videos on YouTube, transcribes them, selects short excerpts, edits those excerpts into vertical short-form clips with captions and an on-screen attribution credit to the original creator, and publishes the resulting clips to the operator's own YouTube and TikTok accounts.

Source content and attribution

Only source videos explicitly licensed as Creative Commons by their original uploader are used. Every published clip includes an on-screen credit to the original creator and a description containing an attribution line and a link to the source video, consistent with the Creative Commons license terms.

Account use

The tool publishes only to accounts explicitly connected and authorized by their owner via OAuth. It does not access, post to, or act on behalf of any account it has not been explicitly authorized for.

The tool and its output are provided as-is, without warranty of any kind. The operator is not responsible for third-party platform policy changes, API changes, or content removal decisions made by YouTube or TikTok.
